Katakat:

  1. Mutton/kidney,brain,heart,chops.
  2. Onion
  3. Garlic
  4. Tomatos
  5. Ginger
  6. Greenchili,Coriander
  7. Lemon juice
  8. Yogurt
  9. Oil,ghee,butter
  10. Katakat Masala

Steps of cooking:

  • Blanch kidney,brain,heart and chops in boiling water for 3 minutes, then remove and cutsmall pieces.
  • In a pot,add already cut pieces of kidneys,heart,chops,ginger,garlic and katakat masala with half cup of water.cover and cooks for 5-10 minutes at low flame.
  • Now add oil/ghee/butter,onion,tomato,yogurt,greenchili,Coriander and brain. fry for 10 minutes on high flame.remove when Oil separates.

Serving Suggestion:

  • Garnish with green Coriander and squeeze lemon juice for enchaced taste.
    • Serve with naan or chapati.
    • Serving for 5-6 people. {1 packet 50g}

    Preparation Time:

    • 35 minutes
